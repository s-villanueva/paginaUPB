Artificial Intelligence Projects

The Advanced AI Project is the capstone course for AI Engineering students. It provides an opportunity to apply deep learning, computer vision, and NLP techniques to solve complex, real-world problems. Students work on high-impact projects that often require GPU acceleration and innovative algorithmic approaches.

Research Proposals

face

Emotion Detection & Facial Recognition

Building a real-time system to identify individuals and analyze micro-expressions to determine emotional states. Applications include personalized user experiences, security systems, and psychological research tools.

traffic

Vehicle Detection & Traffic Heatmaps

Developing a computer vision pipeline to detect and track vehicles in urban environments. The system generates dynamic heatmaps to visualize traffic density, helping to optimize signal timing and urban planning.

auto_awesome

Generative AI for Creative Content

Exploring the capabilities of GANs and Diffusion models to generate high-quality images, music, or text. This project investigates the intersection of AI and creativity, focusing on tools for artists and designers.

medical_services

Deep Learning for Medical Diagnosis

Development of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to detect anomalies in medical imaging such as X-rays or MRI scans. The project focuses on improving diagnostic accuracy and providing interpretable results.
