WELCOME TO

On this site you'll find information about various courses related to engineering programs and the projects carried out within them. Here you can consult the official syllabi, the learning objectives, the recommended bibliography, and the details of each course, which will give you a clear view of the content and the competencies you will develop during your training.

Additionally, this space brings together the research and practical-application projects promoted by the university, showing how the knowledge acquired in the classroom is transferred to real solutions in industry and society. The purpose is to provide you with an organized and accessible academic resource that supports your professional journey and fosters autonomous and collaborative learning.
