Selected Topics in Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to the "Selected Topics in Artificial Intelligence" course. In this intensive 6-week course, we will explore some of the most recent and exciting advances in the field of artificial intelligence (AI). AI is revolutionizing numerous sectors, from healthcare and finance to technology and entertainment. This course is designed to provide you with a deep and practical understanding of the technologies and methodologies that are shaping the future of AI. Students will work with cutting-edge techniques including deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, reinforcement learning, and explainable AI, while also examining the ethical implications of AI development and deployment.

Learning Objectives

  • Deepen understanding of advanced AI concepts: deep learning, NLP, computer vision, and reinforcement learning
  • Develop practical skills through workshops and projects using popular Python libraries and tools
  • Explore practical applications in areas such as healthcare, finance, and recommendation systems
  • Foster critical thinking about the challenges and opportunities presented by AI
  • Understand and apply advanced neural network architectures (CNN, RNN, LSTM)
  • Implement NLP tasks including sentiment analysis, text generation, and automatic translation
  • Develop computer vision systems using convolutional neural networks
  • Implement reinforcement learning agents in simulated environments
  • Apply explainable AI techniques to analyze and interpret ML models
  • Analyze ethical implications of AI development and propose solutions to mitigate risks

Course Outline

    Topic 1: Introduction to AI and Deep Learning
    • Review of basic AI concepts
    • Fundamentals of deep neural networks
    • Architectures: CNN, RNN, LSTM
    • Workshop: Basic neural network implementation in Python
    • Hands-on with TensorFlow and PyTorch

    Topic 2: Natural Language Processing (NLP)
    • Introduction to NLP
    • Language models: Word2Vec, BERT, GPT
    • Applications: automatic translation, chatbots, sentiment analysis
    • Workshop: Sentiment analysis in Python
    • Text generation and language understanding

    Topic 3: Computer Vision and Recommendation Systems
    • Fundamentals of computer vision
    • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for vision
    • Introduction to recommendation systems
    • Content-based and collaborative filtering methods
    • Workshop: Image classification with CNN
    • Building a recommendation system

    Topic 4: Reinforcement Learning and Explainable AI
    • Introduction to reinforcement learning
    • Value methods and policy methods
    • Need for explainable AI
    • Methods to explain AI models
    • Workshop: Implementing a reinforcement learning agent
    • Explaining machine learning models

    Topic 5: Ethics and AI Applications in Health and Finance
    • Ethical considerations in AI
    • AI applications in healthcare and finance
    • Predictive models and ethical considerations
    • Workshop: Predictive models for health and finance data
    • Debate: Ethics in AI development and use

    Topic 6: Final Project Presentations and Reflections
    • Student project presentations
    • Discussion and feedback
    • Summary of covered topics
    • Final reflections and course closure



This Selected Topics in AI course is uniquely positioned to leverage GPU-accelerated computing for cutting-edge AI research and development. Students will utilize campus GPU infrastructure to train complex deep learning models in minutes rather than hours, experiment with large language models like GPT and BERT, and process high-resolution images for computer vision tasks in real-time. GPU computing enables parallel processing of massive datasets, allowing students to work with state-of-the-art architectures including transformers, diffusion models, and multi-modal AI systems. The course emphasizes practical implementation using frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow with CUDA acceleration, preparing students for careers in AI research, machine learning engineering, and data science where GPU computing is essential for developing next-generation AI applications in autonomous systems, medical imaging, financial modeling, and natural language understanding.
