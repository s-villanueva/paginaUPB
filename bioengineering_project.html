Bioengineering Projects

The Bioengineering Project integrates engineering principles with biological systems to improve healthcare and sustainability. Students design innovative solutions ranging from medical devices and bioinformatics pipelines to tissue engineering and synthetic biology applications.

Research Proposals

monitor_heart

Wearable Health Monitoring System

Designing a non-invasive wearable device to continuously monitor vital signs like heart rate variability and oxygen saturation. The system uses edge AI to detect anomalies and alert users to potential health risks in real-time.

insights

Genomic Data Analysis Pipeline

Developing a scalable bioinformatics pipeline to process and analyze large-scale genomic sequencing data. This project aims to identify genetic markers associated with hereditary diseases using machine learning algorithms.

biotech

Micro-Organic Environment Simulation

Developing computational models to simulate microbial interactions within complex micro-environments. This project aims to predict biofilm formation and microbial behavior under varying environmental conditions for applications in bioremediation.

science

Automated Tissue Culture System

Building a robotic platform to automate the maintenance and monitoring of cell cultures in a lab setting. This system ensures precise environmental control and reduces contamination risks for long-term experiments.
